**Teaching Assistant / Support Specialist (Student Position) — Stanford Geospatial Center (SGC)**
The Stanford Geospatial Center (SGC) supports teaching, learning, and research in GIS and remote sensing across Stanford. Guided by the idea that **“Everything is somewhere, and that somewhere matters.”** the SGC provides expertise, tools, and a collaborative space for geospatial work.

We seek a Stanford Doerr School of Sustainability student to provide course-related GIS/Remote Sensing support for students in GIS/Remote Sensing courses and courses with a geospatial component. This position is based in the SGC and is **10–20 hours/week** , depending on student availability.

**Key duties**

* Hold regular open office hours for GIS/Remote Sensing support consultations
* Provide Zoom and in-person support via scheduled appointments (LibCal)
* Provide in-person support and assistance for patrons using the SGC Lab
* Assist with **user management tasks for Stanford’s ArcGIS Online organization** (e.g., account support, basic permissions/role-related help, and onboarding guidance as directed)
* **Proactively identify and catalog Doerr School courses with GIS/Remote Sensing/spatial components** , and communicate with instructors to offer SGC resource onboarding sessions (tools, data, lab workflows, and support options)
* Light maintenance of SGC hardware and software resources
* Assist with workshop/event planning and promotion
* Contribute technical analysis to ongoing SGC projects

**Preferred qualifications**

* Completion of **EARTHSYS 144: Fundamentals of GISci** and/or other Stanford GIS courses
* Experience with at least one major GIS/remote sensing platform (e.g., ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Google Earth Engine, R/RStudio)
* Demonstrated experience applying spatial data analysis within your discipline
* Familiarity with **geocoding, routing/network analysis, and GNSS/GPS field data collection**

**Preferred skills**

* Comfort working in **ArcGIS Online** (e.g., groups, sharing, and basic organization workflows)
